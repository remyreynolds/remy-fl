Bonus Tips
Download MIDI of your favourite tracks and analyze 
them, or at least try recreating them
Use + or - 5 or 7 semitones on oscillators to get a 
more harmonic sound
Learn the rules, then break them
Try splitting your chord progressions between 
different sounds
Chords form the basis of your track, so make sure 
you nail them before moving onto less important 
parts, like mixing and sound design
